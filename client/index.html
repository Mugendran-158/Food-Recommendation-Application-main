      Health Benefits of Seasonal Eating

      Nutritional Advantages

      • Higher nutrient content as produce is harvested at peak ripeness
      • More antioxidants in seasonal fruits and vegetables
      • Better flavor and texture when eaten in season

      Environmental Benefits

      • Reduced carbon footprint from local sourcing
      • Less energy used for storage and transportation
      • Supports local farmers and biodiversity

      Economic Advantages

      • Lower prices due to abundant supply
      • Money stays within the local economy
      • Reduces food waste by aligning with natural cycles

      Seasonal Eating Tips

      • Visit local farmers markets for fresh produce
      • Preserve seasonal foods by freezing or canning
      • Plan meals around what's in season

      Did You Know?

      Eating seasonally can provide up to 40% more nutrients than out-of-season produce that's been stored or transported long distances.
